About Ozempic

What is Ozempic (Semaglutide)?

Ozempic (semaglutide) is a GLP-1 receptor agonist developed by Novo Nordisk. It is the world's most prescribed GLP-1 medication, with an extensive safety record backed by the landmark STEP 1 clinical trial. FDA approved for weight management.

A once-weekly injection that naturally controls appetite and supports sustainable weight management.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the side effects of Ozempic?
Common side effects include nausea, diarrhea, decreased appetite, and constipation. These are usually mild and tend to improve as your body adjusts to the medication. Serious side effects are rare. Please contact us immediately if you experience anything unusual.
Can I get a prescription in just one visit?
Yes. After a consultation with our doctor, you can receive your prescription on the same day if there are no medical concerns. The entire visit takes about 30–45 minutes. You can also have a pre-visit online consultation to save time.
Is there follow-up support after I leave Japan?
Absolutely. You can reach our medical team via WeChat, WhatsApp, or LINE after you return home. We provide ongoing support for side-effect management, dosage questions, and planning your next visit.
Why choose Ozempic?
Ozempic (semaglutide) is the world's most prescribed GLP-1 medication. In the STEP 1 trial, participants lost an average of 14.9% body weight. It has an extensive safety record with millions of patients worldwide.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash (JPY), credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, JCB, AMEX), WeChat Pay, and Alipay.
Can I walk in without an appointment?
We operate by appointment only. Please book via our Google Form or message us through WeChat, WhatsApp, or LINE. Same-day appointments may be available if we have openings.
Can I bring Ozempic home on the plane?
Yes. Ozempic pens can be carried in your hand luggage. We provide a FRIO cooling wallet (free with Standard and Premium plans) to keep the medication at the proper temperature during your flight. We can also issue an English prescription letter for customs if needed.
